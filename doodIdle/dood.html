DOOD Help Page

Stats:

Health: Lose it all and you die!
Strength: This is your maximum damage
Accuracy: This is your minimum damage and affects your ability to hit the enemy.
Armor: This is your damage reduction
Reflexes: This is your ability to dodge enemy attacks
Mana: This is your resource for powerful attacks
Focus Stats: Focus stats are stats that have increased growth and are indicated by the green coloring on the stat in game.

Damage calculation:

Damage is calculated based on multipliers rather than direct Strength vs Armor comparison.
Damage roll is a random number between your strength and accuracy unless your accuracy is higher than your strength, in which case the damage roll will be your strength value.
Defense roll is a random number between your armor and reflexes unless your reflexes is higher than your armor, in which case the damage roll will be your armor.
Damage dealt is calculated via this formula: attacker damage roll * (attacker damage roll / (attacker damage roll + defender defense roll)).
Damage is calculated this way to prevent 0 damage on successful hits.
